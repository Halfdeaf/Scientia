<h2><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: 24px;">About</span> </span></h2>
<p>You start at the Dawn of Time, and have to discover new skills, resources and technologies in order to progress.</p>
<p>Colaborate with villages and rival other settlements on your journey towards understanding the world.</p>
<ul>
<li>Learn Professions and master them to gain access to new abilities and recipes.</li>
<li>The progression is guided by a thorough Advancement system.</li>
<li>Unique items, recipes and dimensions.</li>
<li>A large assortment of both Magic and Tech content.</li>
</ul>
